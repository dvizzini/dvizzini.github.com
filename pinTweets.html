pintweets.com is a hacker project I started last August. It uses Twitter's search and users/lookup API's, along with Google's Maps and Geocoding API's to map the location of Tweet's based on their Tweeter's stated location. Unfortunately, it ceased to work after Twitter's API 1.0 was deprecated. One day I will adapt it to Twitter's latest API.

While the location's mapped are certainly not court-admissible evidence, it was a fun way to get a sense of who is tweeting what around the world.
